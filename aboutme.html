Yaqi Liao (Lillian)
pronounced as [jaˋ tɕʰiˊ, ljɑʊˋ]
Msc. Arch, Aalto University, 2022-current
BDes. Arch, University of Sydney, Australia, 2018-2022

"With a passion for design, Yaqi is always interested in how to make things work whether in a poetic narrative or practical way.

After four years of international study and work in Sydney(UNiversity of Sydney, 2022), Denmark (Aarhus Arkitektskolen, 2019) and Shanghai (Moore Ruble YUdell, 2020), she has an increasing curiosity about the relationship between architecture, humans and the site with honest emotion and critical thinking. Yaqi’s portfolio of work focuses on her narrative and technology-driven projects that endeavour from housing research to public architecture."
