A Home For Christiane F.

Description

A minimal dwelling is designed for the film character Christiane, where the character’s spatial and architectural cinematic universe is analysed and used as a basis for the settlement. Inspired by ‘raumplan’ theory by Adolf Loos, the dwelling created the space three-dimensionally with fully explored timber characters.The project discussed the duality of drug addicts, which derived the design with both introverted and extroverted perspectives.

Date: December, 2019

Location: Mindegade 13, Aarhus, Denmark

Studio: Aarhus School of Architecture Exchange Academic Studio Design

Work Type: Collaborate with Agnes Jarmund
