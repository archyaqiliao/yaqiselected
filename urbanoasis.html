Urban Oasis

Site Analysis

Detail Section

Sectional perspective
Description

Professional athletes faced the repetitive and plain daily training that they worked effortfully to reach the highest point of the profession. The same situation happened to the people who lived in the busy city. They are constantly being pushed and lack space to take deep breaths. The project is a recuperate aquatic centre for professional athletes and residents. As Hetti Perkins described, “ The pool is like an art installation, the light, the sound, the visual appeal of it, the immersiveness of it.” It provides a pleasant swimming experience with environmentally-friendly sustainable systems and natural lighting.

Date: June, 2021

Location: Sydney Pyrmont, NSW, Australia

Studio: USYD Architecture Studio 3A

Work Type: Individual Work
